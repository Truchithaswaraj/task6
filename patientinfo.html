Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I prepare for surgery?

A: Prior to your surgery, our team will provide you with detailed instructions on how to prepare. This may include fasting requirements, medication adjustments, and pre-operative tests.

Q: How can I access my medical records?

A: You can request your medical records by contacting our medical records department. Please allow a few business days for processing.
